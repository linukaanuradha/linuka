Welcome to Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is an island nation located in the Indian Ocean in South Asia, rich in geographical diversity, culture, and biodiversity, with a history spanning over two thousand years. Colombo serves as the commercial capital and plays a key role in international trade, port services, and urban development.

Kandy is a city rich in cultural heritage and attracts devotees and tourists from around the world due to the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic.Sigiriya, as an archaeological site, showcases the engineering and artistic excellence of Sri Lanka’s ancient kingdoms. As a member of the United Nations, Sri Lanka actively participates in international politics, economic affairs, and human rights initiatives.
